Question 6: Social Media Content Matrix.

---

Craft a social media content matrix for a campaign (Choose one of the stages: Awareness, Consideration, Conversion or Loyalty). Make sure that they are related to one another.

Choosing: Awareness

---

A. Goal
   - Make more people in my target audience know about Prompt Alchemist and understand that I teach AI prompting for the future of work.

B. Key Performance Indicators (KPI)
   - Reach and impressions on LinkedIn, Instagram, and YouTube.
   - Growth in followers and profile visits on LinkedIn and Instagram.
   - Views and watch time on awareness videos.

C. Channels
   - LinkedIn (main channel for founders and working professionals).
   - YouTube (for educational awareness videos).
   - Instagram (for short, snackable content).

D. Content Types
   - Short LinkedIn posts and carousels explaining what prompting is and why it matters.
   - Simple YouTube videos introducing basic prompting concepts and use cases.
   - Instagram reels or short clips showing quick prompt tips and results.
