Name the content type for different stages of the social media marketing funnel for your company/brand.

A. Awareness
   - Short LinkedIn and Instagram posts introducing AI prompting and why it matters for the future of work.
   - Simple YouTube or LinkedIn video snippets explaining basic prompt examples.

B. Consideration
   - Longer LinkedIn posts or carousels showing before/after examples of good vs bad prompts.
   - YouTube tutorial videos teaching one clear prompting skill or framework.

C. Conversion
   - LinkedIn posts and emails inviting people to sign up for a paid workshop or course, with clear benefits.
   - Landing page or sign-up page walkthrough videos explaining what they will learn in Prompt Alchemist.

D. Loyalty
   - Follow-up tips, advanced prompt ideas, and “student wins” shared on LinkedIn or email to existing customers.
   - Community content such as Q&A sessions, live streams, or private group posts for past participants.
