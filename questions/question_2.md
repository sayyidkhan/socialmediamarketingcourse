Question 2: Choose Right Social Media Channel/s.

---

Selected Social Media Channels:
  1. LinkedIn – to reach professionals, founders, and knowledge workers where they already talk about work and skills.
  2. YouTube – to teach prompting through tutorials, demos, and long-form educational content.
  3. Instagram – for short-form reels and visual snippets to build awareness and trust.
  4. X (Twitter) – to join AI and tech conversations and share quick prompt tips.

Top Choice:
Linkedin ->
 - LinkedIn has many founders and working adults who want to learn new skills, including how to prompt AI effectively.
 - It helps individuals build my personal brand and promote Prompt Alchemist as a place to learn prompting for the future of work.

Demographics:
  - Age: 23–45 years old.
  - Location: Primarily Singapore, with regional reach to Southeast Asia (Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines) and other English-speaking markets.
  - Occupation: Founders, solopreneurs, working professionals, and students in tech, marketing, business, and operations.
  - Interests: AI, productivity, future of work, entrepreneurship, and digital skills.
  - Income Level: Middle to upper-middle income, with ability and willingness to invest in upskilling, workshops, and courses.

---

Audience:
 - Primary: Founders, solopreneurs, and working professionals aged 23–45 in Singapore and nearby countries who want to use AI better at work or in their business.
 - Secondary: Polytechnic and university students (around 19–25) who are interested in AI, productivity, and the future of work.

Trends in Media Usage:
 - They use LinkedIn to learn about careers, skills, and industry news.
 - They watch YouTube to see step-by-step tutorials and real demos.
 - They browse Instagram for short, fast content and inspiration.

Tone and Style:
 - Simple, friendly, and clear. Avoid very technical or chim language.
 - Practical and action-focused, with real examples and use cases.

Visuals and Design:
 - Clean and easy-to-read slides or carousels showing prompts and outputs.
 - Screenshots or short clips of real AI tools in use.
 - Consistent colours and fonts to build a recognisable brand for Prompt Alchemist.

Copywriting:
 - Short sentences, clear headlines, and simple words.
 - Start with a hook like "Don’t know what to ask AI?" or "Save this prompt for later." 
 - End with a call to action like "Save, share, or try this prompt today."

Frequency & Timing:
 - LinkedIn: 3–5 posts per week on weekdays, mainly during lunch and evenings.
 - YouTube: 1 video per week focused on one clear prompting topic.
 - Instagram: 2–4 short reels or posts per week, repurposed from LinkedIn and YouTube.
